What to do when it's Snowing with no Umbrella

A local survival expert was interviewed today on what to do when caught in a storm without an umbrella. His profound insight shocked everyone when he simply and confidently declared, "Go buy an umbrella".

He further went on to explain that in situations where you feel you would need some sort of cover, going to the nearest store and purchasing a sturdy umbrella is definitely the key to having a more comfortable snow storm experience. When we asked him what someone should do if there was no store nearby he was incredulous and said there is always a store nearby.
"Look," our expert said with a particularly annoyed tone, "if you can't buy an umbrella then I guess you just have to suck it up and walk home cold." He then went on to elaborate about how walking faster can lead to slipping, so you should be cautious but try to get home as quickly as possible. Most of the time you will find a store on your way home and then you can "Go buy an umbrella."
