Forecasters call for weather on Monday

While not specifically say what to expect, one expert claimed to be "reasonably sure" that there would be weather on Monday. Local authorities in disbelief. The level of confidence that the forecasters have in this prediction is unprecedented.

The last time that they had enough confidence to make a declaration of this magnitude was when they declared there would be rain while in the midst of a torrential downpour. Safe to say we are confident about preparing for weather on Monday and would advise you, the reader, to do the same.
