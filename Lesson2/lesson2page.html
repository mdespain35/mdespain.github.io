Design Principles Document

Michael De Spain

Alignment

ESPN

espn.com

This website is a great example of alignment because there are a lot of items that need to be displayed, and they are categorically placed together(various sports leagues) in a visually pleasing way. Everything is aligned in a way that makes the page look organized rather than cluttered.

Visual Hierarchy

Wizards of the Coast

company.wizards.com

This website is a great example of visual hierarchy because right off the rip, you see a massive interactive image as a banner which advertises a product that the company wants to emphasize as well as links to see more.

White Space and Clean Design

Vouch Insurance

vouch.com

This website is a great example of white space and clean design because it has a simple design that flows well and does not have much in terms of items, but doesn't feel empty. The design matches what the company tries to do which is simplify business insurance and it comes through on their page brilliantly.
