Target Audience

The target audience for this website is people who want accurate weather forcasts for either the area they are residing in, or the area they are visiting.

Personas

  1. Richard Roto:
    A young construction worker who needs to know the weather in order to determine his work outfit for the day.

  2. Sally Smallwear:
    A grandmother who wants to take her grandchildren to the park but needs to check the weather first to make sure it will be safe for the kids to play.

Scenarios

  • Family is planning a vacation and they want to know what the weather will be like once they arrive.
  • Student checking the weather before going to school to see if they need a jacket or an umbrella.
  • Commuter checking the weather to see what traffic will be like on their drive home.
  • Foreman at a construction site using the site to decide which day they should pour concrete so it can cure properly.
  • Parents checking the site to see whether they should plan their child's birthday party inside or outside.
